Hi there!

You've reached Sex+, the sexual discovery app for couples.

Too often people are looking to be more adventurous in their sex lives but are afraid to ask in case their partner does not feel the same way.

This is where Sex+ comes in.

How it Works

Take turns to answer a short survey about your sexual preferences. Simply state your level of interest in a sexual activity and Sex+ will combine you responses into a lists of recommendations.

Activities are only recommended when you both give a positive response. This means that only your shared interests will be shown.

Of course, this only works if each partner gives honest responses. You should only continue if you trust each other to do so.

Above all, be safe and enjoy yourselves! ;-)
